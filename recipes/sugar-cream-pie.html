Sugar Cream Pie

Description

This is a delicious sugar cream pie which cooks the filling prior to being put in a baked shell. Real butter and half and half are must-haves for this to be at its best. People always ask me for the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 (9-inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 ¼ cups half-and-half cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Mix cornstarch and sugar
  3. Preheat oven broiler to high
  4. Put under broiler until butter bubbles
  5. Refrigerate pie for at least 1 hour before serving
