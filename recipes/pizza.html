Pizza

Description

This Alfredo pizza recipe combines two of my favorites: chicken and pizza. My husband, who isn't fond of chicken, even likes this!

Ingredients

  • Garlic Butter
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Pizza Dough
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Make garlic butter
  2. Make Alfredo sauce
  3. Make dough
  4. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  5. Prepare chicken
  6. Bake in the preheated about 20 minutes
